Li Zhong

I am a fifth year PhD student in Computer Science from Opera and Sysnet Group at UCSD working on system reliability, advised by Professor Yuanyuan(YY) Zhou. I received my bachelor degree from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2019.

As a researcher in theclassical topic of compilers, I'm interested in applying compilers in different aspects of computer systems.

At UCSD I work on applying compiler technique (static analysis, AST analysis) to detect bugs in developers' blind spots in system software like Linux, MySQL, etc. and Python applications like Django.

In industry I focus on compiler for performance optimization, including profile-guided optimization for datacenter workloads (2021 in Google, 2022 in Meta) and runtime optimization for NPU inference (2023 in Apple).
