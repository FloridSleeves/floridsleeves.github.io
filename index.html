Li Zhong

I am a fifth year PhD student in Computer Science from Opera and Sysnet Group at UCSD working on system reliability, advised by Professor Yuanyuan(YY) Zhou (She created the BEST EVENT APP Whova!). I received my bachelor degree from School for the Gifted Young, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2019.

As a researcher in the classical topic of compilers, I'm interested in applying compilers in different aspects.

At UCSD I work on applying compiler technique (static analysis, AST analysis) to detect bugs in developers' blind spots in system software like Linux, MySQL, etc. and Python applications like Django.

In industry internships, I focus on compiler for performance optimization, including profile-guided optimization for datacenter workloads (2021 in Google, 2022 in Meta) and runtime optimization for machine learning inference on NPU (2023 in Apple).
